About Me

Hello! I am Amitabh Ranjan

Though I am relatively new to this field, I confess its really my "renaissance." Having worked in so many different profiles all these years, from being an analyst with an investment bank, associate admin with tortilla manufacturing company,to being an entrant in the IT field now, I think I have finally found my calling. Throughout my past jobs, there is one innate skill, in particular, about which I have been constantly been made aware of and that is my meticulousness. I believe its a happy marriage of my being meticulous with programming and the love for web development has started to blossom as a result.
Though the COVID-19 pandemic has been a nightmare for majority of the world, I salvaged an opportunity to begin a sojourn that first saw me passing the ASTQB foundation level exam in June 2020 and then the ASTQB Agile test- all on a self-learned basis. But the quench had just started to unfold and so I started learning programming on my own but then realized that I had to break this solitude of self-learning. The idea of -enrolling in a bootcamp was challenging at first but I decided to take the plunge because I needed to streamline my efforts at self-learning. And have never failed to cherish and relish every bit of knowledge gained and assignments undertaken so far.
